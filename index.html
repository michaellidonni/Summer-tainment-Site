Childish Gambino The New World Tour

About the Artist

Donald Glover, born in 1983, is an American writer, comedian, actor, and musician known for his TV series Atlanta and music under the name Childish Gambino. Born in Stone Mountain, Georgia, he attended DeKalb School of the Arts and New York University's Tisch School of the Arts. Glover's music career evolved with mixtapes, albums, and Grammy-winning songs. In 2020, he released the critically praised album 3.15.20, later reissued as Atavista, featuring new songs and collaborations with Ariana Grande and Ludwig Göransson. Glover announced a world concert tour in 2024 and announced his retirement from the Childish Gambino persona.


Info

Childish Gambino's New World Tour is his final concert tour in support of his 2024 albums Atavista and Bando Stone & the New World. The tour will take place in various cities across North America and Europe, with opening acts including Willow in North America and Amaarae in Europe and Oceania. The tour will feature performances at various venues, including Oklahoma City's Paycom Center, Kansas City's T-Mobile Center, and various venues in Canada, the US, and Europe.

Review

Childish Gambino's New World Tour was an amazing experience. The crowd was very energetic, and the stage and special effects were really cool. He performed classics like "Redbone" and "This Is America," along with many new songs from his albums Atavista and Bando Stone & The New World. The concert was extra special because it’s his final tour before he retires from making music, and it was a great way to end off his career. This concert was definitely one of the best I've been to, and it was the highlight of my summer.
